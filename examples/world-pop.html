Example of world map created with geojson2svg

Population wise color coded map. Pink countries with population more than 50,000,000 and skyblue countries with population less than or equal 50,000,000

Countries source geojson's longitude value 180 or -180 is replaced with 179.9999999 and -179.9999999 to avoide the dateline wrap.

In countries population data "United States" is renamed to "United States of America".
